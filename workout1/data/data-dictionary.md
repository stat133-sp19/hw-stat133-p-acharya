*team_name* - name of team 
*game_data* - data of game
*period* - number of period 
*season* - year 
*minutes_remaining* - # of minutes remaining in period
*seconds_remaining*- # of seconds remaining in period
*shot_made_flag* - whether a shot was made or missed 
*action_type* - type of basketball move 
*shot_type* - 2 pointer or 3 pointer 
*shot_distance* - distance to the basket 
*x* - coordinate where shot occured
*y* - coordinate where shot occured
